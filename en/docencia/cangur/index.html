Copa & Prova Cangur

Preparation material for the math competitions organised by the Societat Catalana de Matemàtiques: the Copa Cangur (team-based, ESO) and the Prova Cangur (individual, ESO and Bachillerato).

Copa Cangur

Team-based competition (3 or 4 students) aimed at ESO. Held in January–February at participating schools in Catalonia.

Team-based · ESO

Prova Cangur

Individual competition with multiple-choice problems. Organised by SCM in March, with levels from 1st ESO up to 2nd Bachillerato.

Individual · ESO + BTL
