Collatz and symbolic dynamics

Ongoing doctoral research under the supervision of Dr. Jaume Franch Bullich (UPC). The aim is to build a rigorous theoretical framework for the Collatz conjecture using binary encodings and congruent fibres.

preprint in preparation 2024 — 2026

Modular Fractal Dynamics and the Collatz Conjecture

Manuel Alejandro Reyes Jiménez — Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya

\[T^m(n) = \frac{3^s \cdot n + R(C^m_k)}{2^m}\]

Abstract

Given a positive integer n of level m and a binary encoding Cmk recording the parity of each intermediate step of the Collatz function, we define the iterate Tm(n) via the central expression of the paper. The exponent s counts the odd steps in the sequence and R(Cmk) is given by an explicit summation formula in powers of 3 and 2.

The central hypothesis is that every number of level m reaches a lower level in at most 2m steps of Tm. The paper develops the structure of congruent fibres associated with each binary encoding and analyses the fractal distribution of the dynamic blocks modulo 6.
